# 📌 About

This project presents a strategic roadmap designed to accelerate Spotify's growth by combining business analysis, product thinking, customer research, and market insights.

The objective is to identify scalable growth opportunities while improving customer experience, engagement, and revenue generation.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze Spotify's current business model
- Study the music streaming industry
- Understand user behavior
- Improve user acquisition
- Increase Premium subscriptions
- Enhance customer retention
- Expand monetization opportunities
- Develop a scalable Go-to-Market strategy
